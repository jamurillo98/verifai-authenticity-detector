● ABOUT

About VerifAi

VerifAi is a web-based AI image authenticity checker built to help people navigate a modern problem: it's becoming harder to tell what's real online. With AI-generated images spreading rapidly across social media, news feeds, and messaging platforms, quick verification helps reduce misinformation and rebuild trust.

By combining signal-based analysis (pixel patterns, noise characteristics and compression artifacts) with metadata checks (EXIF verification), VerifAi provides a clear verdict and a confidence score, so users can make more informed decisions before sharing, reporting, or relying on an image.

Group project by Mim Kumar Gurung, Jerome Murillo and Maleesha Hirushan Rashmika. Submitted to Australian Catholic University.
